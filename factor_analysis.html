Choosing the Right Variables

Factors by Variance

The factors with the most influence on variance were area_mean, perimiter_mean, area_se, perimiter_worst, and area_worst.

Scree Plot

The scree plot represents plotted Eigen values, which measure the amount of variance accounted for by each factor. Based on this plot, the ideal number of factors to extract is 5; this is based on where the plot bends and begins to plateau.

Correlation Chart

Dark blue values represent negative correlations between factors whereas the dark red values represent a positive correlation.
